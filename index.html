Our Story

A Taste of Arabia
in Kerala

Nestled along the Palakkad–Kozhikode Highway in Kondotty, Arabian Majlis has been bringing the rich culinary heritage of the Arabian Peninsula to the heartland of Kerala since 2018. From the moment you step in, you are transported to an authentic majlis experience.

Our kitchen is dedicated to time-honoured Arabian recipes — smoke-kissed Alfaham, fragrant Mandi, and rich Mazbi — all prepared with the finest spices and freshest ingredients. We call it "Arabic but with a Twist" — familiar soul, bold innovation.

Kondotty's first choice for authentic Arabian cuisine, with thousands of loyal guests and a story that keeps growing.
